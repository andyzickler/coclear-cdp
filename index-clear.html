Carbon Intensity & Life Cycle Emissions

CDP Data

Over 275 Companies from 79 countries representing 42 global industry category sectors (GICS) participated in the product disclosure question 2016 and 2017. These companies provided life cycle emissions data on over 506 products. The data collected from the questionnaire provides an opportunity for CoClear to make available deeper insight into sector and product-level emissions. By following carbon intensity trends in each sector the CoClear analysis opens a door into efficiency opportunities that can be shared within each sector.
